MAURITIUS CAMPUS

ALU’s inaugural campus is known as African Leadership College (ALC) in Mauritius. ALC offers accredited Undergraduate Degree Programmes through its founding academic partner, Glasgow Caledonian University.

#TheIslandIsMyCampus

The campus opened its doors to its first class of students in October 2015 with 173 students in the inaugural class of 2015. Today we have over 300 students.

ALC's main campus was established in early 2018 in Pamplemousses, Mauritius' northern region, midway between the capital city, Port Louis, and the lively expat district of Grand Baie.

Our students may immerse themselves in a diverse culture with historical roots in Asia, Africa, and Europe, all while living on a tropical island that is frequently recognized as one of Africa’s safest and most education-driven countries.

Academics

ALC offers undergraduate degree programmes through our founding academic partner, Glasgow Caledonian University. Our degree programme has been carefully selected to around what we believe are Africa’s greatest opportunities and grand challenges.
